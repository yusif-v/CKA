# CKA - Certified Kubernetes Administrator

## Exam Overview

Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) exam certifies ability to install, configure, and manage Kubernetes clusters. Administered by CNCF. Duration: 2 hours. Practical, hands-on.

## Key Details

- Kubernetes version: v1.34 (verify latest).
- Domains and Weights:
  - Cluster Architecture, Installation & Configuration: 25%
  - Workloads & Scheduling: 15%
  - Services & Networking: 20%
  - Storage: 10%
  - Troubleshooting: 30%

## CKA Curriculum Hierarchy

- **Cluster Architecture, Installation & Configuration**
- **Workloads & Scheduling**
- **Services & Networking**
- **Storage**
- **Troubleshooting**
